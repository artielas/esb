Webb, Adrian

Full Name: Adrian Webb

Research Description: Distorta caro syndrome, a hereditary genetic mutation that causes the flesh and bone to become malleable when the individual undergoes physical or emotional stress.

History: Adrian Webb was a chemistry student born in Sheffield in 1784. He ran away to London in 1801, and began college in 1802, where he studied his own mutation in secret. He went missing and was presumed dead in 1804.
This information can be found in The Peculiarity of Adrian Webb by Dr. Ernest Adams, as published in 1821.
